The Brains (and lack thereof)

Founded by two friends who decided making games was slightly more fun than their day jobs. One of us is good at art, the other one *thinks* they're good at code. We'll let you decide which is which.

Our philosophy is simple: create cozy, charming games that feel like a warm hug on a rainy day. Also, games with questionable physics engines because that's just funny. We're powered by coffee, questionable life choices, and the sheer belief that someone out there wants to play a game about a sentient turnip accountant.

Follow our journey into madness!
